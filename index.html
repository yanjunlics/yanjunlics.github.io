Biography

Dr. Yanjun Li is currently a Machine Learning Scientist at Calico Life Sciences, an Alphabet-founded research and development lab for human aging. His research interest spans the fields of deep learning, AI-driven drug discovery, structural genomics and proteomics, and precision medicine. Li's work aims to develop innovative AI algorithms to tackle the foundational life science challenges with a broad scientific impact, and optimize and automatize the real-world drug discovery and design pipeline.

Dr. Li was a senior research scientist at the Institute of Deep Learning, Baidu Research USA, Sunnyvale, California. He received his Ph.D. in computer science from University of Florida (2021), advised by Dr. Dapeng Oliver Wu and Dr. Xiaolin (Andy) Li. He received his dual M.E. from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China and Waseda University (2015) and B.E. from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (2012).
